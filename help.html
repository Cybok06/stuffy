Help and support

Stockly helps you manage your boutique inventory efficiently. All data is stored locally on your device for offline use.

Tips

  • Regularly export your data as backup (see Sales history → Export CSV).
  • Keep product quantities updated after restocking or sales.
  • Use clear product names so searching is fast during checkout.
  • Watch low-stock counts and restock early.
